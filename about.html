What makes our coffee special.

Since opening our first coffee shop near the Pamantasan Lungsod ng Muntinlupa. Brew Ha? was founded by John Lloyd Diaz , Roselyn Rubillo and Lorania Lei Hernando Jr. in 2023. With similar coffee philosophies, it was only time before they got together to start Brew Ha?.

Our baristas to get to know everyone that stops by the shop. Don't be surprised if we know your name, remember your regular drink order, or dish out high-fives across the bar. Coffee is about connecting people. but are driven ahead knowing that our coffee provides fuel for others blazing their own paths.
